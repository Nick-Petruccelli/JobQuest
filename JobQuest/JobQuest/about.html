Our Story

We are a team of four university computer science students working together on our class project, JobQuest. Our mission is to help users discover computer science career paths that match their skills, interests, and preferences.

This project is a result of collaboration, creativity, and passion for technology. We're excited to share our work with you and hope it helps guide your career journey.
